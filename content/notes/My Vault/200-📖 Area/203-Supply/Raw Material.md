## Pet food (dry)

2. Liquid

#### Receiving Methods
1. Bulk Receiving 散装车 - goes to RM silos or Inbound silos
2. [[Pneumatic Delivery]] 气送
3. Big Bag 吨袋 (500kg-1ton) - goes to 大袋闸
4. 袋装 (5-25kg) - goes to RM silos
5. 油罐车 (3-4 ton/receiving)
6. 吨桶
7. 小桶
